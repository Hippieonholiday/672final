About Us

A little about our history

Guido's Restaurant, in West LA, has been serving up authentic Northern Italian cuisine incorporating the freshest ingredients and traditional family-style recipes passed on from generation to generation.

Our History

For over three decades, our family has been proud to serve Los Angeles with authentic Northern Italian cuisine. What began in 1990 as a small trattoria has blossomed into one of LA's most beloved Italian dining destinations.

Started by Guido Andrade after immigrating from Northern Italy, our restaurant carries forward the culinary traditions passed down through generations. Using his grandmother's cherished recipes and techniques learned in his family's kitchen in Italy, Guido created a menu that brings the true taste of authentic Italian cooking to Los Angeles.

A Family Tradition

Today, the second and third generations of our family continue to honor Guido's legacy. Each dish we serve is a testament to our commitment to authentic Italian cooking, using the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques that have been passed down through our family.

From hand-made pasta to our signature sauces, every item on our menu tells a story of tradition, family, and passion for great food.

Chef's Inspirations

Our kitchen is led by Executive Chef Marco Andrade, who learned the art of Italian cooking directly from his father, Guido. Trained in both traditional techniques and modern culinary arts, Marco brings a perfect blend of old-world authenticity and contemporary creativity to our menu.

Each season, our chef creates special dishes inspired by our family's Northern Italian roots, incorporating the finest local California ingredients. From our handmade gnocchi to our carefully crafted risottos, every dish reflects both our heritage and our innovation.

Our Philosophy

At our restaurant, we believe that great food is about more than just ingredients—it's about family, tradition, and the joy of sharing a meal together. We maintain strong relationships with local California farmers and Italian importers to ensure we're using the finest ingredients available, just as Guido did when he first opened our doors.

Every guest who dines with us becomes part of our extended family, and we strive to make each visit feel like coming home to an authentic Italian dinner.

Our Community

For over 30 years, we've been more than just a restaurant—we've been an active part of the Los Angeles community. From supporting local schools to participating in neighborhood events, we believe in giving back to the city that has supported us for so many years.

We're proud to have served multiple generations of LA families and to have been part of countless celebrations, family gatherings, and special moments in our customers' lives.
